Who Am I?

My Study History

I am currently learning to code at Coder Academy as a beginner. I have always been more of a logic-based thinker than a creative type - I preferred mathematics and science at school over essay writings and art. Even during University I studied a Bachelor of Science majoring in Psychology - but I never thought to explore tech side of the world until I was stuck at home like everybody else due to the pandemic. I was eager to challenge myself for self-growth during this time, so I took the opportunity to take free introductory courses on multiple fields. One of the courses I came across was a web development class and I was instantly drawn to it. From then I have been self learning using free online programs like freeCodeCamp, Dash and codecademy. While it is quite challenging and frustrating at times, I an throughly enjoying the learning process, especially now that I am attending Coder Academy.

My Personality & Interests

In the past couple of years I discovered my love for travel. After graduating form The University of Melbourne, I left Australia for a year long travel to Japan, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia and Canada, and I truly loved every moment of it.

I also love the little things in life, like drinking coffee in the morning, listening to some music on the train, putting makeup on, sitting on the grass and feeling the breeze...etc. I believe they give you time to breathe and let your mind wander off, so I have been appreciate these little moments.
